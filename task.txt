Core In-House Components

1. General Ledger (GL)
- Chart of Accounts (multi-entity, multi-currency)
- Journal Entry engine
- Period & fiscal year management
- Trial Balance
- Financial statements (BS, P&L, Cash Flow)
- Audit trail & posting rules
- Recurring entries
- Accruals & reversals

2. Accounts Payable (AP)
- Vendor management
- Invoice processing & matching (2-way, 3-way)
- Payment scheduling & execution
- Vendor aging reports
- Early payment discounts
- Debit memos & adjustments
- AP reconciliation

3. Accounts Receivable (AR)
- Customer management
- Invoice generation
- Receipt & payment allocation
- Credit management & limits
- Collections workflow
- Dunning management
- AR aging & DSO tracking
- AR reconciliation

4. Multi-Entity & Intercompany
- Entity hierarchy management
- Intercompany transaction engine
- Intercompany eliminations
- Transfer pricing rules
- Shared services allocation
- Minority interest tracking

5. Group Consolidation
- Consolidation rules engine
- Currency translation (CTA)
- Elimination journals
- Segment reporting (IFRS 8 / ASC 280)
- Ownership structure management
- Consolidation audit trail

6. Multi-Currency
- Currency master & exchange rates
- Rate source management (daily, monthly, spot)
- Revaluation engine
- Unrealized/realized FX gains & losses
- Currency translation adjustments
- Triangulation (for multi-leg conversions)

7. Fixed Assets
- Asset register
- Depreciation engine (straight-line, declining balance, units of production)
- Asset lifecycle (acquisition → disposal)
- Revaluation (IFRS model)
- Impairment tracking
- Asset tagging & location tracking
- Capital WIP (Work in Progress)

8. Cost & Budgeting
- Cost center management
- Cost allocation rules
- Budget creation & versioning
- Budget vs actual tracking
- Forecasting engine
- Variance analysis
- Project costing

9. Period Close & Reporting
- Close checklist & workflow
- Soft & hard close controls
- Sub-ledger to GL reconciliation
- Financial reporting engine
- Report builder (P&L, BS, CF by entity/segment)
- Comparative reporting (prior period, prior year)
- Drill-down from report to journal

10. Audit, Compliance & Controls
- Immutable audit log
- Segregation of duties (SoD) engine
- Approval workflows (multi-level, threshold-based)
- Document attachment & retention
- SOX control mapping
- Period lock management
- User activity tracking
